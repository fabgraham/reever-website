About Reever

We're a four-piece pop/rock cover band formed in 2024, delivering a mix of pub hits from the '80s, '90s, '00s, and beyond. We’re experienced musicians who get a crowd moving with a blend of songs that everyone knows and loves.
Based in Woking, we're set to bring our contagious energy to pubs, private events and festivals across Surrey and the surrounding areas. If you’re looking for a band that will have you singing, dancing, and rocking out, Reever is your go-to band for a night filled with the best pop/rock vibes!

  • We're ready for weddings, birthdays, pubs, & corporate events
  • We've got al the equipement needed, including PA and lighting
